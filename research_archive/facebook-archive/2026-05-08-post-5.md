# Post 5 — 2026-05-08

## Content

Now that AI is being trusted to send emails and phone calls, look through texts, all that kind of stuff, if you don't hear from someone for a few days, you might want to remember these simple old-fashioned rules of communication:

A. Write an Email.
B. Send a text message.
C. Call and leave a detailed voicemail.

These three rules could help you keep your communications from being lost in this horribly confusing world of AI, just as they helped me keep my communications from being lost with my young assistants throughout my 27-year career.
